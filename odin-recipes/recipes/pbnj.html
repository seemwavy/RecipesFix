Peanut Butter & Jelly Sammiches recipe

Make sandwiches so good it will make your neighbor climb through your window to join in.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly

    Instructions

  1. Take two slices of bread out of their bag. Lay them flat.
  2. Use a butter knife to slather peanut butter on one piece, then jelly on the other.
  3. After both slices are covered with their respective condiment, put the two slices together on the condiment side.
  4. Enjoy
