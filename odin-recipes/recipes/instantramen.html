Instant Ramen Recipe

How to make microwave Ramen that's better than ya mama's.

Ingredients:

  • Dried Ramen Pack
  • Water
  • Bowl

    Steps

  1. Put dried ramen into the bowl then add water. Be careful not to overfill.
  2. Boil water in microwave for 2-4 minutes
  3. Remove bowl from microwave. (Be careful, it's hot!)
  4. Drain excess water, then stir in flavor packet to the ramen.
  5. Enjoy!
